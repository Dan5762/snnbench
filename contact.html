Behind the Scenes

The Spiking Neural Network (SNN) Benchmarks are a comprehensive collection of SNN performance from leading research papers. This initiative is maintained by Daniel Long, an enthusiast and researcher in the field of computational neuroscience and neuromorphic computing. The goal is to track the leading approaches and the current state of the art in spiking neural networks.

If you want to contribute to this effort or simply have a chat don't hesitate to get in touch by email.
